<h2>2. What We Collect and Why</h2>
<h5 class="mt-3">a. Website Analytics (Legitimate Interest)</h5>
<p>
When you visit our site, we automatically collect limited technical information to help us monitor performance,
improve usability, and maintain security.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Pages viewed, clicks, and time on page</li>
<li>Browser type, screen size, and language</li>
<li>Referrer (if any)</li>
<li>Country and city (derived from your IP address)</li>
<li>A <strong>hashed version of your IP address</strong> (stored to detect unique visits)</li>
<li>Session ID and device information (non-identifiable)</li>
</ul>

<p>
This data helps us understand site usage and detect errors or abuse.
No raw IP addresses or personal identifiers are stored.
</p>
<p>Analytics data are retained for <strong>up to 6 months</strong>, then automatically deleted.</p>

<h5 class="mt-3">b. Contact Form (Consent)</h5>
<p>
If you use our <strong>Contact Us</strong> form, the information you provide is sent directly to us by email.
It is <strong>not stored</strong> on the website or in any database, and is used only to respond to your enquiry.
</p>
